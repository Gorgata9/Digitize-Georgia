We provide the complete Design, Development (Coding), and Hosting of your first Home Page at zero cost for the first year. There are no monthly subscription fees for this service. Our goal is to remove the financial barrier for Georgian businesses entering the digital market.

A Home Page is a single-page digital storefront. It acts as a professional business card that works 24/7. It typically includes:

  • Hero Section: Who you are.
  • Gallery: Photos of your products or services.
  • About: Your story and location.
  • Contact: Phone, WhatsApp, and Social Media links.

We can help! While the standard Home Page is free, we offer affordable upgrade packages for businesses ready to expand. Whether you need a full E-commerce shop, a hotel booking engine, or a multi-page corporate site, we offer competitive rates exclusively for our Georgian partners.
