Everything you need to know about our free website program for Georgian businesses.

We provide the complete Design, Development (Coding), and Hosting of your first Home Page at zero cost. There are no monthly subscription fees for this service. Our goal is to remove the financial barrier for Georgian businesses entering the digital market.

A Home Page is a single-page digital storefront. It acts as a professional business card that works 24/7. It typically includes:

  • Hero Section: Who you are.
  • Gallery: Photos of your products or services.
  • About: Your story and location.
  • Contact: Phone, WhatsApp, and Social Media links.

We can help! While the standard Home Page is free, we offer affordable upgrade packages for businesses ready to expand. Whether you need a full E-commerce shop, a hotel booking engine, or a multi-page corporate site, we offer competitive rates exclusively for our Georgian partners.

You only need to pay the annual fee for the domain (approx. 30 GEL/year). We will set it up for you.

We welcome all Georgian businesses. Whether you are a guesthouse in Svaneti, a wine cellar in Kakheti, or an artisan shop in Tbilisi, if you have a product or service to offer, you are eligible.

We strongly encourage using authentic photos of your actual business. While stock photos look "perfect," they are often less effective because customers trust reality.

Showing your real team, workshop, or products, even if the photos are taken with a smartphone, it creates a genuine connection that leads to better results than generic internet images.
