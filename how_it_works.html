THE PROCESS

How It Works

From selection to launch, here's how we transform local Georgian businesses into digital success stories.

1

Selection & Strategy

We identify local brick-and-mortar businesses—cafes, artisans, and service providers—that lack a modern digital presence. After a brief consultation to understand their story, we map out a professional, SEO-friendly landing page.

2

Development & Mentorship

Each project is assigned to a front-end development intern. Under the direct supervision of Developers Alliance senior leads, the intern builds a responsive, high-performance website. This ensures the business gets a "senior-grade" product while the intern gains verifiable, real-world experience.

3

Launch & Hosting

We don't just build it and leave. We handle the technical heavy lifting:

  • • Domain Registration: Securing a professional web address.
  • • Secure Hosting: Providing a stable environment so the site stays online.
  • • Google Business Integration: Ensuring the business is "on the map" and searchable.
4

Community Growth

Once live, the business has the tools to compete in the digital economy. The intern leaves with a professional portfolio piece and a refined skill set, and Tbilisi's local economy becomes a little more resilient.

5

Scalability & Growth

Your Digital Roadmap

Our goal is to help you outgrow your initial landing page through free e-commerce trainings offered by our members and partners. As your business expands, your digital needs will evolve—whether that involves automated AI booking agents, integrated inventory management, or a full-scale e-commerce storefront.

Transitioning to Professional Services

When you are ready for high-complexity systems, we offer a seamless transition to our senior team at Developers Alliance. At this stage, your project moves from a volunteer-led initiative to a dedicated professional service category, ensuring you have the infrastructure to scale globally.

The Power of Choice

Our mission is your success, not a locked-in contract. You are under no obligation to continue with our paid services; your site is yours, and you are always free to transition to any agency of your choice. We're just happy to have built the foundation for your growth.

REQUIREMENTS

Eligibility Criteria

To ensure we are helping the businesses that need it most and providing our interns with high-quality projects, we look for partners who meet the following criteria:

Physical Presence

You are a "brick-and-mortar" business, artisan, or entrepreneur primarily operating within Georgia (with a focus on the Tbilisi community).

The Digital Gap

You currently have no website, or your existing site is severely outdated (e.g., "pre-COVID" information, non-responsive design, or broken links).

Commitment to Accuracy

You can provide the necessary high-resolution photos, up-to-date pricing/menus, and basic business details (hours, location, contact info) to ensure the site is useful to customers.

Scalability Alignment

You are a small-to-medium enterprise. While we love large-scale operations, this specific initiative is designed for those who do not currently have the budget for a full-scale agency.

Collaborative Spirit

You are willing to work with our intern-led team, providing feedback during the development process to help them grow while they help you go digital.

Ready to Get Started?

If you meet our eligibility criteria and are ready to take your business digital, we'd love to hear from you.
