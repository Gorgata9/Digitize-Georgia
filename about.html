DIGITIZE GEORGIA PROJECT

Digitize Georgia is an initiative dedicated to empowering local businesses across the country by providing professional web presence at zero cost. We believe that every Georgian entrepreneur, from the mountains of Svaneti to the vineyards of Kakheti, deserves access to world-class digital tools.

Our mission goes beyond building websites. We're creating a digital ecosystem where traditional craftsmanship meets modern technology, where family businesses can reach global audiences, and where the spirit of Georgian hospitality shines through every pixel.

By removing the financial and technical barriers to online presence, we're not just helping individual businesses grow ‚Äî we're strengthening the entire Georgian economy, one home page at a time.
